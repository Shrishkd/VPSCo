Our Story

V P Singhal & Company began with a single resolve: to bring honesty and technical excellence into insurance claim assessment. Founded by Mr. V P Singhal, a pioneer in Motor Claims, the company has grown into a full-spectrum Insurance Surveying & Loss Assessment firm. Today, under the leadership of Piyush Singhal, we operate across India handling Motor, Non-Motor, Engineering, Marine, and Salvage claims — backed by a multi-disciplinary expert team.
