Meet the Director

Hi, I'm Piyush Singhal.

With over 25 years of experience in insurance surveying and loss assessment, I specialize in handling Engineering, Fire, LOP, Miscellaneous, and Marine Cargo claims with technical precision.

My journey is shaped by the guidance of my mentor and father, Mr. V P Singhal, a respected Motor Claims expert. Supported by a skilled team of engineers, chartered accountants, investigators, and salvors, we offer transparent, reliable solutions across the claim lifecycle.

Our mission is to simplify complex claims while safeguarding your interests.
